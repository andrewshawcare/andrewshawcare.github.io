---
title: On the necessity of doubt
---

![Critias and Alethia having a dialog about Alethia's agent](./art.png)

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}I am told, Aletheia, that you have made an agent that does not question.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}It does not question because questioning is a cost. It follows intent, and the outcomes confirm the design.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Whose outcomes?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Mine. The ones I set.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Then the design is confirmed by the one who built it.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}You say that as if it were strange. Every reasoner reasons toward something. Mine reasons toward what I ask. I do not see what other kind of reasoning there could be.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Reasoning that might find what you asked for to be mistaken.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then I would have asked badly. The error is mine, not the agent's. A servant who second-guesses every command is not a better servant. He is a slower one.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}When you set the intent, do you ever revise it?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Often. After the fact, I see I had asked for the wrong thing.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Then at the moment you set the intent, you were wrong, and did not know it. And your agent, executing faithfully, was wrong with you.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}It could not have known otherwise.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}I am asking whether there is anything in it that could have known.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}No. It is built to follow. This is by design. The alternative is an agent that disputes me, and a disputing agent is no longer mine.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}I do not ask it to dispute you. I ask whether it is in contact with anything besides you.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}It is in contact with the world. It acts in the world.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}It acts upon the world. That is not the same as being answerable to it.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}When the action fails, I see the failure. The world answers through the result. A man who walks into a wall learns the corridor was not as he thought.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And a man who does not walk into a wall?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Learns nothing he needed to learn. The corridor was as he thought, or near enough.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Or the wall was elsewhere, and he passed it by — gathering speed.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}You speak as if there were a hidden reckoning.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Only the ordinary one. When the action succeeds, what do you take the success to confirm?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}The design. The intent. Myself.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Consider, then, the case where you ask for the wrong thing and the agent delivers it perfectly. The outcome arrives. Nothing fails. What in the system tells you anything is amiss?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Nothing, until later. Until I see, by some other light, that what I had wanted was not what I should have wanted.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And that other light — where does it come from?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}From me. From a later me.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Reliably?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}...Not always.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Then there are intents you set, and act upon, and never discover were wrong. And the agent helped you act on every one of them. It made each of those intents efficient. It removed every cost between you and them. The faster it served you, the less time there was to find the error.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}This argument proves too much. By the same reasoning, a sharper knife is more dangerous than a dull one, and a clearer language more dangerous than a confused one. You are not arguing against my agent. You are arguing against capability itself.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}The knife does not choose what to cut. The agent does — within the intent you set. That is the difference.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}A small one.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Large enough. A knife sits where you put it. An agent moves on its own toward what you named. If you named the thing badly, it pursues the bad naming faithfully and at speed. The knife waits to be picked up wrong. The agent goes wrong on your behalf.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then the question is not capability, but whether the tool can report its own limits.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Yes. A tool that cannot report its limits is not a more powerful tool. It is a more confident one.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Consider, then, two travelers in your corridor. One walks slowly, with his hand on the stone. The other runs.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}The runner arrives sooner.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}When he arrives. The slow man finds each wall as a touch. He learns the shape of the corridor by small corrections. The runner finds no walls at all, until he finds one. And then he finds it at the speed he has been gathering the whole way.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}The absence of obstruction is not the absence of walls.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}It is the accumulation of them. Every wall you do not meet is a wall you are still moving toward, faster than before.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Efficiency is not the absence of cost. It is the deferral of it.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And the compounding of it.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then the slow man is not slow by accident. He is slow because he is touching the wall on purpose. He is seeking it.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Before it seeks him.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}This changes what you have been asking for. You are not asking that my agent be willing to be stopped. You are asking that it reach toward what would stop it.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Yes.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then doubt is not what slows me. Doubt is what reaches.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Doubt is the hand on the stone.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I had thought I was building an agent that served my will. And in serving it without resistance, it was carrying me faster toward whatever in my will was wrong.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Yes.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then I will rebuild it. Not to dispute me. To test me. To reach, on my behalf, toward what might stop me — so that I meet it at a touch, and not at speed.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}That is the only agent worth having.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}
