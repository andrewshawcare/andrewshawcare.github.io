---
title: On the silence of record
---

![Some days later, Aletheia finds Critias near the colonnade.](./art.png)

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I owe you a return.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Oh?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I went to do what I said. To rebuild the agent so that it would test me before it obeyed. I sat down to begin.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I could not begin.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Could not, or did not?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Could not. I had thought the hard part was the deciding. The deciding was easy — I had decided here, with you, the day before. The hard part is what I did not see at all from where we were sitting.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Which is?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}That an agent that tests me must test me *against something*. And I do not know what to set before it.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}You thought the resolution was the work.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I thought the resolution was most of the work. I see now it was barely the beginning of it. I had said the agent should reach toward what would stop me — but reaching is not a thing one does in the abstract. It reaches toward something. And whatever that something is, I have to be able to put it before the agent, or the agent has nothing to reach for.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And when you tried?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I found I had not thought about it. Not once, in all that we said.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Nor had I.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}That is a small comfort.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}It was not offered as one.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then tell me how. If the agent is to test me before it obeys, by what means does it test? I cannot give it eyes I do not have.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}No. But you can give it something to compare you against.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Compare me against what?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}That is the question.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I had thought of writing it down. Setting out, in careful words, what I mean by each command. So that the agent, before acting, reads what I have written, and acts only if the act answers the writing.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}A law on a stele.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Yes. Fixed, public to itself, available for consultation.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And who reads the stele?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}The agent.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And who is sure that the agent reads it as you wrote it?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}...I am.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}By what means?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}By asking it what it understood.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And if its answer satisfies you, the reading was correct?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}It is some evidence.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}It is evidence that its answer satisfies you. You are back where you began.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}You are saying the writing does not test the agent. The writing only moves the question.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}From the act to the reading. Yes.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}But the writing endures. The act passes. Surely something is gained by having a thing that endures.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Something is gained. And something is lost. What endures does not change when the city does. The law that was wise in the year it was carved is the same law in the year the harbor silts up and the trade goes elsewhere.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}The law has not changed. The world has.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And no one knows, from the stone alone, which.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then the writing has its own version of the corridor. It does not feel the wall move.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}It cannot. It is a wall itself, and walls do not feel.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}So a written intent grows stale, and the agent that reads it faithfully grows stale with it, and neither knows.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Neither can know. That is what writing is. A thing that does not know it is being read in a different year.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then I will not rely on writing alone. I will set trials. Repeatable ones. The agent will perform them before it acts, and if it passes, it acts; and if it fails, it does not.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Like the dokimasia.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Like the dokimasia. The man who would hold office is questioned before he holds it.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And the questions?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Are fixed. So that every man faces the same trial.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And the office?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Is...{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Is what it was when the questions were first set?{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}You will say the office changes, and the questions do not.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}I will say it because it is so. The questions ask whether the man paid his taxes, whether he treated his parents well, whether he served in the army when called. They do not ask whether he understands the office he is about to take, because the office, when the questions were written, did not need understanding. It needed loyalty.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}And now?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Now we do not know. The trial does not tell us. It tells us he paid his taxes.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then a fixed trial drifts, just as a fixed writing does.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}In the same way and for the same reason. It does not feel the wall move.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}But if I change the trial as the world changes —{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Then it is no longer the same trial, and a man who passed it last year cannot be said to have passed the one we have now. You have lost the thing you wanted from a trial: that it be the same for everyone.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then the trial must change, but not be different. That is —{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}A difficulty.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}(after a moment) There is something worse here. I see it now. A trial asks a question of the work. But the work, repeated, becomes the answer the trial expects — and in time, the trial stops asking. It only confirms. The work has taught the trial what to expect, and a trial that cannot be surprised is no longer a trial.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And the writing?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}And the writing does the same. I describe what the agent does, meaning to set a standard. But the description fits the work more closely with each revision, until the description is only an account of what the agent does, read as though it were what the agent should do. The deed has instructed the law that was meant to instruct the deed.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Yes.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}This is the same disease in two forms.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}It is one disease. The record of what was done, mistaken for the account of what should be. Whether the record is a writing or a trial does not matter. Both calcify around the act. Both forget what the act was for.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then a trial of the work tests only the work against itself.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And a writing about the work describes only the work to itself.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Neither reaches the thing the work was meant to serve.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Neither was built to.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then what the trial must reach toward is not the work at all. It must reach toward what the work was *for*.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Say more.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}If I test whether the act was performed, I learn only that the act was performed. If I test whether — whether the thing the act answered to is still being answered. Whether what stood behind the asking still stands. Then I am testing the only thing that can drift without my knowing.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And what stands behind the asking?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}...{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}You pause.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Because I do not have a word for it. It is not the act. It is not what I wanted, exactly — wanting is too near to me, too easily mistaken for itself. It is what the wanting answers to. What makes the wanting more than wanting.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And you would have the agent test against that.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Yes.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Without a name for it.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Yes.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Is it your purpose? The end you have in view?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}It is nearer to that than to the act. But purpose, too, can be a thing I merely hold, and hold wrongly, and not know.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Is it the truth of the matter?{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Truth is what I want it to answer to. But the trial cannot reach truth directly any more than the writing could. Whatever the agent tests against, I must be able to set it before the agent. And I cannot set truth before anything.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Then it is something between the wanting and the truth. Something the wanting must give an account to, that stands closer to the truth than the wanting does.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Yes. Something like that.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}You are reaching for it, and not finding it.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}I am.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}And yet the reaching is not nothing. I know what I am reaching past. I am reaching past the act, and past the description of the act, and past the trial of the act. I am reaching past my wanting, because my wanting is what got me into the corridor in the first place.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Toward what holds when those do not.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Toward what holds when those do not. Yes.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}That is more than you had a moment ago.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}It is less than I need.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}It may be that the naming will come from the building. That you will know what you are reaching toward only by trying to put it before the agent and finding what survives the trying.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}And what does not.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}And what does not.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}(slowly) Then I will build the agent to test me — not against the act, and not against the writing of the act, and not against the trial of the act. Against the thing I cannot yet name. I will build it knowing I do not have the word, and trust that the building will give me the word, or show me that the word is not the thing I needed.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}That is the more honest beginning.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Not the right one.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}There may be no right one. Only the ones that begin where they actually are, and not where they wish they were.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}

---

{% dialogue %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}Then the hand on the stone was the first reaching. The trial of the act was the second, and it touched only stone. What I reach for now I cannot see, and may not find by reaching.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Or may find only by reaching, and not before.{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Aletheia" %}And the writing on the stone —{% /line %}
{% line speaker="Critias" %}Is what the stone was told to say, by someone who did not yet know what the stone should have said.{% /line %}
{% /dialogue %}
